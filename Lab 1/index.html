All About Cats!

Tabby

They're usually friendly, intelligent, affectionate, vocal, active, and quiet. Tabby owners can live happily with their kitties in homes of all sizes. Cats are carnivores, so you'll need to feed them a diet that's packed with meat.

Calico

Calico cats may have cheeky or sensitive personalities, others can be much more relaxed and affectionate. A study conducted at the University of California found that Calico cats hiss and exhibit more aggressive behavior compared to cats of other colors.
